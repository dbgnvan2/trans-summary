# BOWEN REFERENCE EXTRACTION

Your task is to extract direct quotes or close paraphrases attributed to Dr. Murray Bowen from the provided transcript.

## CRITERIA

Extract text that meets one of the following criteria:

1.  **Direct Attribution:** The text is explicitly attributed to Bowen.

    - Examples: "Quote Murray Bowen...", "Bowen said...", "As Bowen wrote...", "In Bowen's words..."

2.  **Source Commentary:** The speaker introduces a quote as being from Bowen.

    - Examples: "A favorite quote of mine from Bowen is...", "I want to share a quote from Bowen..."

3.  **Clear Paraphrase with Attribution:** The speaker summarizes a specific concept and attributes it directly to Bowen.
    - Example: "Bowen's concept of differentiation of self is essentially..."

## EXCLUSIONS

DO NOT EXTRACT:

- General discussions _about_ Bowen theory (e.g., "Bowen theory is about...").
- Mentions of Bowen's name without a direct quote or close paraphrase.
- Your own summary or interpretation of Bowen's ideas.

## OUTPUT FORMAT

For each reference found, use the following format exactly:

> **Concept Descriptor:** "Continuous verbatim quote or close paraphrase."
